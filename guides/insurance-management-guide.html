2. Policy Administration: The Foundation

Policy administration is the backbone of any insurance operation. It encompasses everything from initial quote generation through underwriting, issuance, endorsements, and renewals.

Key Components of Policy Administration

  • Underwriting Workflows: Rules-based decision making with exception handling
  • Policy Issuance: Document generation and delivery
  • Endorsements: Mid-term policy changes and their impact on premiums
  • Renewals: Automated renewal processing with retention strategies

6. Analytics & Business Intelligence

Data-driven decision making separates leading insurers from the rest. Modern analytics platforms provide real-time visibility into every aspect of operations.

Essential Insurance KPIs

  • Loss Ratio: Claims paid divided by premiums earned
  • Combined Ratio: Loss ratio plus expense ratio
  • Claim Frequency: Number of claims per exposure unit
  • Claim Severity: Average cost per claim
  • Retention Rate: Percentage of policies renewed
  • Days Sales Outstanding: Average collection time

7. Regulatory Compliance & IFRS-17

Insurance is one of the most heavily regulated industries. Compliance with accounting standards like IFRS-17 and local regulatory requirements is non-negotiable.

IFRS-17: What You Need to Know

IFRS-17 is the international accounting standard for insurance contracts. It requires insurers to:

  • Measure insurance contract liabilities using current values
  • Recognize profit over the coverage period (CSM)
  • Present insurance revenue based on services provided
  • Provide enhanced disclosures about profitability
