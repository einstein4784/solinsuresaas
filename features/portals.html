Self-Service Portals

Empower agents, brokers, and policyholders with branded self-service portals. 24/7 access to quotes, policies, claims, and more.

Digital-First Experience

Sol-Insure's Portal module delivers modern, responsive self-service experiences for all your stakeholders. From agents quoting and binding policies to policyholders filing claims, every interaction is streamlined and intuitive.

Each portal is fully customizable with your branding, and role-based permissions ensure users see exactly what they need. Mobile-optimized design means access from any device, anywhere.

Portal Types

Tailored experiences for every user

Agent Portal

Full quoting and binding capabilities, book of business management, commission tracking, and marketing materials. Streamline the entire agent workflow.

Broker Portal

Multi-carrier comparative quoting, client management, and production reporting. White-label options available for broker branding.

Policyholder Portal

View and download policy documents, make payments, request changes, file claims, and track claim status. Self-service reduces call volume.

Vendor Portal

Repair shops, medical providers, and service vendors can view assignments, submit invoices, and track payments.

Mobile Apps

Native iOS and Android apps for policyholders and agents. Push notifications, photo capture for claims, and digital ID cards.

White-Label Branding

Complete customization with your logo, colors, and domain. Each portal reflects your brand identity.

Policyholder Self-Service

Empower policyholders to manage their insurance online:

  • View and download policy documents and ID cards
  • Make one-time and recurring payments
  • Request policy changes and endorsements
  • File and track claims with photo upload
  • Update contact information and preferences
  • Access 24/7 chatbot support
