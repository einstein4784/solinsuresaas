Accurate Reserve Estimation

Sol-Insure's Reserve Management module provides actuaries with powerful tools for loss reserve estimation and analysis. From traditional development methods to advanced stochastic models, our platform supports the full range of reserving techniques.

Integrated with claims data in real-time, your reserve analysis always reflects the current state of your book. Automated workflows for quarterly reserve reviews ensure consistency and compliance.

Key Capabilities

Comprehensive actuarial tools for reserve management

Triangle Analysis

Build and analyze loss development triangles with flexible aggregation options. Paid, incurred, and reported triangles with automated data population.

IBNR Estimation

Multiple IBNR methods including Chain Ladder, Bornhuetter-Ferguson, Cape Cod, and Generalized Linear Models. Compare and blend results.

Case Reserve Management

Track and manage individual case reserves with approval workflows. Bulk reserve tools and automated reserve adequacy reviews.

Stochastic Models

Bootstrap and simulation-based methods for reserve variability analysis. Generate confidence intervals and risk distributions.

Reserve Review Workflow

Automated quarterly and annual reserve review processes. Documentation, sign-off, and audit trail for regulatory compliance.

Regulatory Reporting

Generate Schedule P exhibits, actuarial opinions, and loss development exhibits. NAIC and state-specific formats supported.

Supported Estimation Methods

Comprehensive toolkit for actuarial analysis:

  • Chain Ladder (Development Factor) Method
  • Bornhuetter-Ferguson Method
  • Cape Cod (Stanard-Bühlmann) Method
  • Generalized Linear Models (GLM)
  • Mack Bootstrap for variability
  • Frequency-Severity models
