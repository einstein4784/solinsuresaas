End-to-End Policy Lifecycle

Sol-Insure's Policy Administration module provides a comprehensive platform for managing the entire policy lifecycle. From initial quote generation through binding, endorsements, renewals, and cancellations, every step is streamlined and automated.

Built on a modern, cloud-native architecture, our policy admin system handles complex multi-line, multi-state products with ease while maintaining the flexibility insurers need to compete in today's market.

Key Capabilities

Everything you need to manage policies efficiently and accurately

Quote Management

Generate accurate quotes in seconds with real-time rating, eligibility checks, and automated underwriting rules. Support for comparative quoting across multiple products.

Binding & Issuance

Streamlined binding workflow with automated document generation, e-signature integration, and instant policy issuance. Real-time validation ensures accuracy.

Endorsements

Process mid-term changes efficiently with automated premium calculations, pro-rata adjustments, and instant document regeneration. Full audit trail maintained.

Renewals

Automated renewal processing with intelligent pricing, risk reassessment, and proactive customer engagement. Configurable renewal rules and workflows.

Cancellations

Handle cancellations with proper premium calculations, regulatory compliance, and automated notifications. Support for flat, pro-rata, and short-rate methods.

Document Management

Automated generation of all policy documents, declarations, and forms. Version control, digital signatures, and secure document storage included.

Product Configuration

Build and maintain complex insurance products without coding. Our intuitive product configurator allows you to:

  • Define coverage types, limits, and deductibles
  • Configure rating algorithms and factors
  • Set up underwriting rules and eligibility criteria
  • Create state-specific variations and forms
  • Manage product versioning and effective dates
