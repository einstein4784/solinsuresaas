Key Capabilities

Comprehensive reinsurance administration

Treaty Management

Maintain all treaty terms, layers, and conditions. Support for quota share, surplus, excess of loss, and catastrophe covers.

Automated Cessions

Real-time cession calculations as policies are written. Automatic application of treaty terms, retentions, and limits.

Facultative Placement

Manage facultative submissions, quotes, and bindings. Track multiple markets and negotiate terms within the system.

Bordereaux Reporting

Automated generation of premium and loss bordereaux. Configurable formats to match reinsurer requirements.

Claims Recovery

Track reinsurance recoverables on claims. Automated billing to reinsurers and payment reconciliation.

Financial Reporting

Ceded premium and loss reports, aging of recoverables, and reinsurer performance metrics. Schedule F support.

Treaty Types Supported

Handle any reinsurance structure with our flexible configuration:

  • Quota Share with sliding scale commissions
  • First and Second Surplus treaties
  • Per Risk and Per Occurrence Excess of Loss
  • Aggregate Excess of Loss and Stop Loss
  • Catastrophe covers with multiple layers
  • Multi-year and multi-currency programs
