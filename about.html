Who We Are

▶ Our Story

/ Mission & Vision

Sol-Insure is a next-generation, cloud-native SaaS platform designed specifically for P&C insurers, MGAs, and digital insurers. Built from real-world experience in the Caribbean and LATAM markets, Sol-Insure bridges the gap between heavy enterprise systems and outdated legacy software — offering insurers a modern, scalable, reliable alternative that delivers full operational control at a fraction of the cost.

Sol-Insure was founded to solve a widespread industry problem: mid-size insurers are forced to choose between expensive enterprise platforms with overwhelming complexity or low-end systems that lack compliance, automation, and scalability. We built Sol-Insure to be the perfect middle ground — a powerful, modular, cloud-native platform designed for emerging markets and insurers who demand agility, speed, and modern capabilities.
