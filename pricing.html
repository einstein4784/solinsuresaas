Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Business and Enterprise plans include complimentary implementation support valued at $5,000.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers. Enterprise customers can also arrange purchase orders and net-30 terms.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Experience the full platform before committing.

How does data migration work?

We provide data migration services for all plans. Starter includes self-service tools, while Business and Enterprise get dedicated migration specialists.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use AES-256 encryption. Your data is hosted on AWS with multi-region redundancy.
