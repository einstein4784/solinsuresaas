▶ Overview


Sol-Insure is a fully integrated P&C core administration platform that runs entirely in the cloud. It manages the complete insurance lifecycle — from underwriting to policy issuance, billing, claims, reinsurance, accounting, regulatory reporting, and analytics. Built for emerging markets and insurers who demand agility, speed, and modern capabilities.

▶ End-to-End Flow

/ Complete Lifecycle

▶ Process Flow


1. Product Setup: Configure rating, underwriting rules, coverage structures, taxes, fees, and documents.


2. Customer & Broker Engagement: Onboard customers, capture information, and issue quotes through web-based portals.


3. Policy Issuance: Automatically generate, save, and distribute policy documents via customer and broker portals.


4. Billing & Collections: Automated installments, receivables, commissions, and payment workflows.


5. Claims Management: Complete FNOL to payment workflow with adjuster assignment, investigation, reserving, and recovery.


6. Reinsurance Processing: Integrated treaties, cessions, bordereaux, and recovery calculations.


7. Accounting & Finance: Premium accounting, claims accounting, commission accounting, and GL export.


8. IFRS-17 & Reporting: CSM, LRC, LIC, disclosure reports, actuarial export, and regulatory filings.
